Instructions for sharing KeyShotVR

Below is your KeyShotVR. To view the KeyShotVR by itself open the 3D VR_VR.161.html file in the 3D VR_VR.161 folder.

 

Uploading to and displaying on your website:

Upload the '3D VR_VR.161' folder with all of its contents to your webserver. Copy (Ctrl-C/Cmd-C) and Paste (Ctrl-V/Cmd-V) the code below to your webpage.

[Code]: 

<div style="max-width: 1920px; max-height: 1080px;">
  <div style="left: 0px; width: 100%; height: 0px; position: relative; padding-bottom: 56.25%; overflow: hidden;">
    <iframe src="3D VR_VR.161.html"
                 allowfullscreen
                 style="position: absolute; top: 0px; left: 0px; height: 100%; width: 1px; min-width: 100%; *width: 100%;"
                 frameborder="0"
                 scrolling="no">
    </iframe>
  </div>
</div>

If you share your KeyShotVR via email, send the entire zipped '3D VR_VR.161' folder to the recipient(s).
